How to use a NTUC Fairprice voucher?

  1. Access the e-voucher by clicking on the URL.
  2. E-voucher will load on a browser (not required to download the app).
  3. E-voucher is ready for use in stores or online. E-voucher status and QR code will be reflected.

Note: Further instructions on how to use the e-voucher and terms & conditions can be found upon clicking on the URL

Redeem in stores:
Pay via the FairPrice app

  1. Add vouchers at Payment page. Tap ‘Add’ next to Voucher code.
  2. Use your stored vouchers. Select one or more vouchers you would like to use.
Or

  1. Manually enter Voucher code. Copy and paste or enter your code into the ‘Enter voucher code’ field and proceed to checkout.

At Cashier

  • • Open the available voucher in your app or mobile browser, and show the QR code to the cashier.

At Self-Checkout Counter

  • • Open the available voucher in your app or mobile browser. When selecting payment type at the kiosk:
  •  ✓ Select All Other Payment.
  •  ✓ Select Partner Voucher / FP eVoucher.
  •  ✓ Use the handheld scanner to scan the app QR code.

Redeem Online:

  1. Add vouchers at Checkout. Click ‘Add’ next to Voucher code. Shop online at fairprice.com.sg OR the FairPrice App.
  2. Use your stored vouchers. Select one or more vouchers you would like to use.
    3. Or
  3. Manually enter Voucher code. Copy and paste or enter your code into the ‘Enter voucher code’ field and proceed to checkout.
